Welcome to Guidr!

Do you seek new paths? New portals? Scenery away from the ordinary?

Whether it's outdoors or urban exploration-- Guidr has everything you need: A treasure trove of exploration and sights unseen, for the modern adventurer.

Your answer to wanderlust

Guidr is a new social media platform that allows users to create their Outdoor Resume of all travels, trips, trails, and tracks.

Our Outdoor Resume is a digital passport that allows users to become both guide and follower: Sharing their travels and locations. This sharing allows fellow adventurers to find new destinations and locations, across the globe.
